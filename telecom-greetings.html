Telecom greetings

When branding your business on the telephone - we answer the call!

When a potential customer finds you, there's a good chance they may want to --- wait for it --- CALL YOU! Yep - actually use the phone to call you. And, if your telecom system has Auto Attendant greetings to distribute incoming calls, and/or on-hold messaging, the voice and music on those recordings better be extraordinary, because they're representing your business! That's where we can help...


Our professionally recorded custom telecom greetings:

  • Increase your marketing effectiveness
  • Improve your presentation on the phone

Auto Attendant Greetings (IVR / ACD / Mailbox):

Auto Attendant is an effective way of offering information to your callers, while reducing the flow of call traffic to your agents. An engaging voice(s), along with a well-crafted script, increase the probability of a caller fully navigating through your prompt system, and gaining the information they need, rather than pressing "0" and defaulting to an agent. This helps you save both time and money!

On-hold Messages:

Callers waiting in queue become a "captive audience". We help you take advantage of that valuable wait time with our ear-catching on-hold messages that uniquely brand and dynamically market your business, thus helping turn callers into money-spending customers!

Outbound Broadcast Messages:

A strategically selected voice(s), and even music bed, make these messages more receptive, while providing greater impact when cold-calling potential prospects.

Why Us?

Our voice-over talent team ranges from kids to mature adults. We also feature multicultural dialects and many different languages, along with script writing, editing and translation services.


Telecom voice-over is a specialized form of audio production because each phone system is somewhat unique unto itself. We can format our greetings to match your system's technical specifications, and produce messages that are clear and pleasing to the ear!
